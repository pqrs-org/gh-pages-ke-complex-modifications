Mouse keys Ambidextrous

Replace the mouse with your keyboard, with either or both hands. With fast scrolling, and two speed settings.

Hold the left_control key in tandem with W,A,S,D (left hand) or I,J,K,L(right hand) for mouse movement.

Hold shift + left_control for 4x faster mouse movement (max speed).

Fn + W,A,S,D (left hand) or I,J,K,L(right hand)for scrolling.

Key bindings:
  • Mouse motion: left_control + W,A,S,D or I, J, K, L
  • Fast mouse motion: left_control + shift + W,A,S,D or I, J, K, L
  • Mouse left click: left_control + Q or U
  • Mouse right click: left_control + E or O
  • Mouse scroll: Fn + W,A,S,D or I, J, K, L
